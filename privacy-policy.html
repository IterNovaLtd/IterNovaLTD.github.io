10. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any right or submit a Subject Access Request, email hello@iternova.co.uk. We will respond within one month, per ICO guidelines.

11. Security Measures

  • Role-based access controls in SharePoint/OneDrive.
  • Regular backups, retention-label enforcement, and disaster-recovery procedures.
  • Mandatory annual GDPR & data-security training for staff.
