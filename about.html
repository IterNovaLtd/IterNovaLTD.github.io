About iterNova

Based in Brighton, iterNova is a boutique consultancy dedicated to helping insurers and growth-minded organisations innovate and excel. By blending strategic frameworks with creative experimentation and AI-driven solutions, we deliver robust processes, on-time results and customer-centric experiences that scale.

From streamlining claims workflows to launching new digital products and building 24/7 customer-service bots, we work side-by-side with you to co-create solutions tailored to your needs. Our mission? To turn smart ideas into real impact—faster, smarter and with lasting value.
